# Welcome to the docs!

## Input Format:
Number(Space)Operator(Space)Number
eg.: -1509 x 75757

## Funktions:
+ = Addition
- = Subtraction
x = Multi.
* = Multi.
/ = Division
^ = Power
v = Roots; "2 v n" for Squareroot of n; works not only with Squareroots; all floats are supported
tan & tanh
sin & sinh
cos & cosh
log
% eg.: 25 % 80 = 20
